ABOUT US

Company story

Wander Nippon was born from a shared passion for the hidden beauty of Japan. Founded by two lifelong friends who met under Kyoto’s cherry blossoms, the company set out to show travelers more than just the postcard sights.

Learn more

Mission state

To create unforgettable travel experiences across Japan by combining authentic cultural immersion, personalized service, and deep local knowledge. We aim to connect travelers with the heart of Japan—its people and traditions.
